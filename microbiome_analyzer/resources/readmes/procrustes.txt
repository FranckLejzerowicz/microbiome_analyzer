It is obtained using the PROcrustean randomization TEST (PROTEST), a permutation-based method to assess the significance of the observed M^2 value.  This involves randomly shuffling row assignments in one matrix many times to generate a null distribution of M^2 values, allowing for statistical inference.\n\nIt is done in QIIME2 using the q2-diversity library",
